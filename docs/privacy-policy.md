# Vinayaka Transport Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-23

## 1. Scope
This Privacy Policy explains how Vinayaka Transport collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data across customer, rider, tracking, operations, and admin experiences.

## 2. Data We Collect
- Account data: name, phone number, email (where applicable), role.
- Order data: pickup and drop addresses, booking details, parcel metadata.
- Operational data: trip status, tracking timeline events, delivery proof metadata.
- Device and technical data: IP address, request metadata, timestamps, auth events.

## 3. How We Use Data
- To authenticate users and secure account access.
- To create, assign, and fulfill transport bookings.
- To provide shipment tracking and status updates.
- To support operations, dispute handling, and service analytics.
- To comply with legal obligations and prevent misuse/fraud.

## 4. Legal Basis
Data processing is performed for service delivery, legitimate business operations, user-requested actions, and legal compliance.

## 5. Data Sharing
We share data only with:
- Internal authorized staff for operations and support.
- Infrastructure/service providers required to run the platform.
- Legal authorities when required by law.

We do not sell personal data.

## 6. Security Controls
- Role-based API authorization controls.
- Token-based authentication.
- Audit logging for sensitive administration actions.
- Input validation and server-side sanitization on key flows.

## 7. Data Retention
Data is retained only as long as necessary for service delivery, dispute resolution, legal obligations, and security monitoring.

## 8. User Rights
Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data, subject to lawful retention requirements.

## 9. Children
The service is not intended for children under the minimum legal age in applicable jurisdictions.

## 10. Contact
For privacy requests or concerns, contact: support@vinayakatransport.example

## 11. Policy Updates
We may update this policy periodically. Material changes are reflected by updating the date above.
